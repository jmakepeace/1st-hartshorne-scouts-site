Our sections

All young people welcome

At 1st Hartshorne we run sections for young people from age 6 to 14. Whatever age your child is, there is a place for them here.

We believe every young person deserves the chance to discover their potential — to make friends, try new things, and have a brilliant time doing it. Our volunteers are fully trained and DBS checked.

  • 🦫 Beavers 6–8 years
  • 🐻 Cubs 8–10½ years
  • ⚜️ Scouts 10½–14 years

Meeting times

When and where we meet

All meetings run weekly during term time. Please get in touch before your first visit so we can expect you.

🦫 Beavers Ages 6–8 Monday evenings 5:45pm – 7:00pm St Peter's Church Hall
🐻 Cubs Ages 8–10½ Friday evenings 7:00pm – 8:30pm St Peter's Church Hall
⚜️ Scouts Ages 10½–14 Tuesday evenings 7:00pm – 9:00pm St Peter's Church Hall

All meetings run during term time only.

Join us

Interested in joining?

Complete the form to register your interest and we will get back to you to arrange your first visit. We aim to reply within a few days.

  • Open to all genders, backgrounds and abilities
  • Qualified, DBS-checked adult volunteers
  • Meetings run weekly during term time
  • Uniform and kit guidance provided on joining

Thinking about volunteering?

We're always looking for adults to help lead our sections. No Scouting experience is needed — just enthusiasm. Get in touch and we can tell you more.

If you have already submitted via the Scout Association website, you don't need to fill this form in as well.
