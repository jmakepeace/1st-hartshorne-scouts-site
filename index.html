Our sections

All young people welcome

At 1st Hartshorne we run sections for young people from age 6 to 14. Whatever age your child is, there is a place for them here.

We believe every young person deserves the chance to discover their potential — to make friends, try new things, and have a brilliant time doing it. Our volunteers are fully trained and DBS checked.

Based in Hartshorne near Swadlincote, we welcome families from across South Derbyshire and the North West Leicestershire border — including Woodville, Midway, Newhall, Church Gresley, Ashby-de-la-Zouch, Blackfordby, Moira, Boundary, Repton and Ticknall.

What we do

Skills for life, every week

From outdoor adventures to team games, our weekly sessions are packed with hands-on things to do. Each section enjoys activities and challenges suited to their age, plus badge work, friendships and plenty of fun — all led by trained, DBS-checked volunteers.

Days out, camps and special events run alongside the regular programme, so there's always something to look forward to.

Camps and adventures

Outdoor skills, confidence and memories

Scouting is not just about weekly meetings. Our young people get the chance to camp, explore outdoors, learn practical skills and take part in adventures throughout the year.

From pitching tents to cooking outdoors and spending time with friends, camps are a brilliant way to build confidence and independence.

Join us

Interested in joining?

Complete the form to register your interest and we will get back to you to arrange your first visit. We aim to reply within a few days.

  • Open to all genders, backgrounds and abilities
  • Qualified, DBS-checked adult volunteers
  • Meetings run weekly during term time
  • Uniform and kit guidance provided on joining

Thinking about volunteering?

We're always looking for adults to help lead our sections. No Scouting experience is needed — just enthusiasm. Get in touch and we can tell you more.

Already contacted us through scouts.org.uk? You do not need to fill this form in as well. If this is your first enquiry, use the form below and we will get back to you.

By submitting this form you agree to us using the details you've provided to contact you about joining 1st Hartshorne Scout Group. We won't share your information with anyone else and we'll only keep it for as long as we need to respond to your enquiry. If we go on to register your child, we'll let you know separately about the Scout Association's own privacy notice.

What happens next?

After you enquire

We reply to your enquiry

One of our volunteers will contact you to confirm the right section and talk through availability.

You arrange a first visit

We will let you know when to come along, what to expect, and anything your child needs to bring.

We share joining details

If your child joins, we will explain registration, subs, uniform and the information we need from you.

If there is a waiting list

If a section is full, we will explain the current position and keep your enquiry on record.
